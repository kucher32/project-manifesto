# Examples

Real-world adoptions of `project-manifesto`, anonymized where needed.

This folder is intentionally sparse at launch. Examples will be added as projects adopt the framework and their founders contribute back.

---

## Contributing an example

If you have adopted this framework and want to share your experience:

1. Open an issue describing your project (without sensitive details)
2. Discuss what to include
3. Open a pull request with an anonymized version of your `PROJECT.md`, `TODO.md`, or session log structure

What to anonymize before sharing:
- Brand names (if private)
- Specific customer or partner names
- Pricing details
- Production endpoints
- Anything classified as CONFIDENTIAL or SECRET in your project

---

## Suggested example formats

- **`example-saas-mvp/`** — a SaaS in pre-MVP phase
- **`example-marketplace/`** — a two-sided marketplace
- **`example-internal-tool/`** — a non-commercial tool
- **`example-research-project/`** — academic or research context

These are placeholders — real examples will replace them.
